# Sales & Production Dashboard

## Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales, production, and customer data. It helps managers and analysts monitor revenue, costs, product performance, and customer trends for informed decision-making.

## Key Features / Diagrams

1. **Sum of Price and Sum of Production Cost by Product Name**  
   - Compares total sales revenue and total production costs per product.  
   - Useful to evaluate profitability and margins.

2. **Sum of Units Sold by Product Name**  
   - Displays total quantity sold for each product.  
   - Helps identify top-selling products and inventory trends.

3. **Customer Purchase Breakdown**  
   - Visualizes distribution of purchases among customers.  
   - Highlights most valuable customers or segments.

4. **Product Purchased by Date**  
   - Shows product sales over time.  
   - Useful for spotting trends, seasonality, or peak sales periods.

5. **Sum of Total Purchased by State**  
   - Aggregates total purchase amounts per state.  
   - Helps identify geographic sales patterns.

6. **Sum of Production Cost by Product Name and Price**  
   - Displays total production cost alongside sales price per product.  
   - Enables analysis of product margins and cost-effectiveness.
